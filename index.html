Why buy through Red Sea Sails

A factory yacht. A local team.

Buying a yacht from abroad is the easy part. Landing it, flagging it and keeping it sailing in the Red Sea is where most buyers get stuck — that's exactly what we handle. We've done it for our own fleet of four Hanse yachts since 2015.

Turnkey delivery to Egypt

Order, ocean freight, customs, commissioning and handover — managed end to end, so you take delivery on the water, not at a foreign yard.

A berth, and a base

Moorage, maintenance and a crew who know your boat in Hurghada, Soma Bay and El Gouna — plus charter management if you want the yacht to earn.

Into the sailing scene

Owners step straight into the Red Sea's racing and events calendar — the Soma Bay festival, the El Gouna regatta and a growing one-design community.
